Goa


Goa is one of the most popular holiday destinations in India, known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, Portuguese heritage, and delicious seafood.

  1. Baga Beach is famous for nightlife
  2. Calangute Beach is known as the queen of beaches
  3. Fort Aguada offers amazing sea views
  4. Dudhsagar Falls is a stunning waterfall
  5. Basilica of Bom Jesus is a historic church
  • Goan Fish Curry is very flavorful
  • Prawn Balchão is a spicy seafood dish
  • Chicken Xacuti is rich and aromatic
  • Bebinca is a traditional dessert
  • Feni is a local drink
